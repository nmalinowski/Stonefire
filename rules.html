How to Play Stonefire

Stonefire is a turn-based card game. Each player starts with 30 health, a deck and mana which increases.

Turn Structure

  1. Draw a card
  2. Gain mana
  3. Play cards and attack
  4. End turn

Victory

Reduce the opponent's hero to 0 health.
